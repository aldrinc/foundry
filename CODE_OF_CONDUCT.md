# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors and maintainers to act professionally and
respectfully.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement.
- Assume good faith, but be direct about bugs, risks, and evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and abusive conduct.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory or exclusionary behavior
- Deliberate disclosure of private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict
participation for behavior that undermines a safe and productive working
environment.
